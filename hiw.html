The SOS feature.
Imagine you are sick in a foreign country. You don’t know how to call an ambulance and are too weak to figure out how to contact your family, boss, insurance company and doctor at home.


  • Customize who you want the SOS button to contact before an emergency happens
  • Choose your personalized message to send to your emergency contacts
  • The app will send your emergency contacts your exact location via GPS so they can come to the rescue
  • The SOS button is easy to access via the TripMD app
  • Don’t know the numbers for local authorities? Don’t worry, your GPS will set the city automatically so you can easily make an emergency phone call

SOS is customizable to your needs.
When you are in a medical emergency, the people you need to contact go beyond your loved ones. Your employers, insurance company and Doctor back home all need to know if you are in a health related crisis.


  • Reach out to all of your emergency contacts with the click of a button.
  • Send personalized messages
  • Set up your SOS feature on a desktop computer so there is no thought requireed during an emergeny
